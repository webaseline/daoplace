    Ultimate VietNamese dining experience like no other
    This story is about a family of three generations with a strong survival spirit and a determination to start their new life in the U.S and in the process found themselves a passion and destination for a culinary tradition. Đào Place is not just another restaurant but a work of love and passion. It is her latest culinary creation with more than 40-year experience behind. Madame Đào, the Founder and Master Chef of Đào Place was a pioneer immigrating to San Francisco in 1975..
